Wampum

Wampum is our cross-platform mobile app currently under development. Based upon a barter system, Wampum lets users interact with each other to discover item matches within specified parameters. It’s as simple as snapping a picture, adding a description, and seeing who else might be up for a swap. Swipe for items you’re interested in. If both people are interested in a trade, you’ll be able to message each other to work out the details. We use geo-location services, scalable backend technologies, and tight security protocols to ensure a stable & secure platform for our service.

Worldwide

The great thing about Wampum is that it's based upon the universal currency of barter: swapping things for other things. This can work for either swapping a coffeepot for a turntable, a guitar pedal for a guitar pedal, or a goat for a chicken.

An Efficient Way To Barter

Traditional bartering requires face-to-face exploration & haggling. Wampum takes this a step away from geographically centric barter: search as far and wide as you want in order to find what you need! Then, communicate with the other party to arrange completion of the deal.
